Projects

Collective Gabiru is an artistic group that develops experimental and collaborative works based on attentive listening and coexistence with the territory. During a three-month residency in Bairro do Rego, in Lisbon, the collective immersed itself in local life to observe, record and reinterpret the symbolic, visual and sound elements of the neighborhood. Through a collective and fluid creative process, the group built works that reflect the cultural multiplicity and urban transformations of the region.
